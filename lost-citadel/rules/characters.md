# Pre-Built Characters — The Lost Citadel

**Fixed stat array: 10, 11, 12, 13, 14, 15**

Assign these six values to STR, DEX, CON, INT, WIS, CHA as you prefer.

Modifiers: 3-4 = -4, 5-6 = -3, 7-8 = -2, 9-10 = -1, 11-12 = 0, 13-14 = +1, 15-16 = +2, 17-18 = +3

---

## 1. FIGHTER — "Swordmaster" (Lawful Warrior)

**Hit Dice:** 1d8 → roll [5] = **5 HP** (with CON mod applied: 5+1 = **6 HP**)

**Armor Class:** 5 (leather armor)

**Starting Equipment:**
- Longsword (1d8 damage)
- Shield (+1 AC)
- Leather armor
- Bedroll, torch, waterskin, 10 gp

**Abilities:**
- **Weapon Mastery:** Longswords. +1 to attack/damage with longswords.
- **Grit:** Choose STR or DEX. Advantage on checks of that type to overcome opposing force (kicking open doors, breaking free of chains, etc.).
- **Hauler:** Add positive CON mod to gear slots.

**Talent Roll (2d6):** Roll for bonus ability at level up.

**Recommended Stats:**
```
STR 15 (+2)    DEX 12 (0)     CON 13 (+1)
INT 10 (-1)    WIS 11 (0)     CHA 14 (+1)
```

## 2. PRIEST — "Holy Warrior" (Lawful Servant of Mercy)

**Hit Dice:** 1d6 → roll [4] = **4 HP** (with CON mod: 4+1 = **5 HP**)

**Armor Class:** 6 (chainmail)

**Starting Equipment:**
- Mace (1d6 damage)
- Chainmail
- Holy symbol (takes no gear slots)
- Bedroll, torch, waterskin, rope (60'), 10 gp

**Abilities:**
- **Turn Undead:** Can turn undead creatures (spell-like ability, doesn't count vs. known spells).
- **Deity:** Serves **Saint Terragnis** (Lawful; patron of justice). She can revoke spellcasting if you act blasphemously.
- **Languages:** You speak **Celestial** (angelic language).
- **Spellcasting:** Cast priest spells you know.

**Known Spells (Tier 1, choose 2):**
- *Cure Wounds* — Heal target for 1d6 HP
- *Light* — Create a light source (torch radius)
- *Purify Food & Water* — Remove poison/spoilage
- *Sanctuary* — Target can't be attacked for 1d4 rounds (DC 12 WIS check by attacker)
- *Turn Undead* — Force undead to flee or cower (bonus ability)

**Talent Roll (2d6):** Roll for bonus ability.

**Recommended Stats:**
```
STR 13 (+1)    DEX 10 (-1)    CON 14 (+1)
INT 11 (0)     WIS 15 (+2)    CHA 12 (0)
```

## 3. THIEF — "Scout & Lockpick" (Neutral Rogue)

**Hit Dice:** 1d4 → roll [3] = **3 HP** (with CON mod: 3+0 = **3 HP**)

**Armor Class:** 7 (leather armor)

**Starting Equipment:**
- Shortsword (1d6 damage)
- Shortbow + 20 arrows (1d6 damage)
- Leather armor
- Thievery tools (built-in, no gear slots)
- Bedroll, torch, waterskin, 10 gp

**Abilities:**
- **Backstab:** Hit unaware targets with extra 1d4 damage.
- **Thievery:** Advantage on climbing, sneaking, hiding, disguising, finding/disabling traps, lock-picking, pickpocketing.
- **Gear Slots:** 6 total

**Talent Roll (2d6):** Roll for bonus ability.

**Recommended Stats:**
```
STR 12 (0)     DEX 15 (+2)    CON 10 (-1)
INT 14 (+1)    WIS 11 (0)     CHA 13 (+1)
```

## 4. WIZARD — "Spell Researcher" (Neutral Scholar)

**Hit Dice:** 1d4 → roll [2] = **2 HP** (with CON mod: 2-1 = **1 HP** — minimum 1)

**Armor Class:** 10 (no armor; vulnerable!)

**Starting Equipment:**
- Dagger (1d4 damage)
- Spell book (contains 3 tier 1 spells)
- Bedroll, torch, waterskin, ink & quill, 10 gp

**Abilities:**
- **Spellcasting:** Cast wizard spells you know.
- **Languages:** Common + **2 additional common languages** + **2 rare languages** (Draconic, Primordial, etc.).
- **Learning Spells:** Can learn new spells from spell scrolls (DC 15 INT check; scroll is used up either way).
- **No Armor:** You're defenseless in melee. Stay back!

**Known Spells (Tier 1, choose 3):**
- *Burning Hands* — 1d6 damage in 15' cone (DC 12 DEX to half)
- *Charm Person* — 1 target, DC 12 WIS or treats you as friendly (1d6 rounds)
- *Identify* — Learn properties of a magical item (1 minute)
- *Magic Missile* — Fire 1d4+1 projectiles, each deals 1 damage (auto-hit)
- *Sleep* — Put creatures to sleep (weakest targets first, 1d4+1 HD of creatures)

**Talent Roll (2d6):** Roll for bonus ability.

**Recommended Stats:**
```
STR 8 (-2)     DEX 13 (+1)    CON 11 (0)
INT 15 (+2)    WIS 12 (0)     CHA 10 (-1)
```

## How to Use

1. **Pick a class** (Fighter, Priest, Thief, or Wizard).
2. **Assign stats** from the fixed array to STR/DEX/CON/INT/WIS/CHA based on recommended distribution.
3. **Roll hit dice** (or use the rolls above) and apply CON modifier.
4. **Write down spells** (for Priest/Wizard).
5. **Note equipment & abilities**.
6. **You're ready to play!**

---

## Death Timer

If you hit 0 HP, you have **1d4 + CON modifier** rounds (minimum 1) before death.

On your turn, roll 1d20. **20 = you stabilize with 1 HP**.

Otherwise, dying continues. An ally can **Stabilize** you with a DC 15 INT check.
