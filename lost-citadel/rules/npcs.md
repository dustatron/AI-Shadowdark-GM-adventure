## Faction Attitudes

### Beastmen Reaction

**Hostile Triggers:**
- Loud noises, combat, breaking things
- Entering Area 18 (Scarlet Minotaur zone)
- Attacking one of their own

**Neutral/Cautious Default:**
- Superstitious about outside world
- Fear "dragons" beyond citadel walls (false belief)
- Can be allied if convinced party is helping defeat Scarlet Minotaur

**Dialogue Example:**
> "Shhhh! You want to wake the beast? It stalks the halls, always hungry. If we can trick it into the bull statues... then maybe we own this place again."

### Ettercap Reaction

**Hostile Triggers:**
- Attacking them or their treasure
- Blocking access to loot

**Neutral Default:**
- Focused on treasure acquisition
- Willing to parlay if treasure respected

**Treacherous Triggers:**
- Showing significant wealth
- Getting trapped between ettercaps and treasure

**Dialogue Example:**
> "Shiny, shiny... you want some? Or you want to DIE for some?"

### Scarlet Minotaur Reaction

**Always Hostile:**
- No negotiation
- Charges on sight
- Never retreats

**Fear Tactics:**
- Can be lured into bull statue traps (Areas 7, 10, 13)
- Charges relentlessly toward heat/light/sound
- Returns to Area 18 after hunts

**Dialogue Example:**
> *Bellowing, snorting, hooves scraping stone... the sound of something ANCIENT and ANGRY heading your way...*

## Combat Tactics

### Beastmen in Combat
1. Try to flee to Area 14 or Area 23
2. Use secret doors (if known)
3. Attempt to lure party into bull statue traps
4. Attack in packs for advantage
5. Avoid Area 18 at all costs

### Ettercaps in Combat
1. Creep on ceilings, maintain distance
2. Use poison web (DC 12 DEX escape)
3. Retreat to Area 4 if outnumbered
4. Gang up to assist allies
5. Reluctant to enter Area 18

### Scarlet Minotaur in Combat
1. Charge headfirst at targets
2. Use horns to gore, hooves to trample
3. Swing greataxe with devastating force
4. Pursue relentlessly
5. Use environment (charge down narrow halls)
6. Never give up, even at low HP
