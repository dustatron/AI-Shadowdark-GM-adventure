# The Lost Citadel — Encounters & Mechanics

## Random Encounter Rules

**Danger Level:** Risky.

**Encounter Checks:**
- **Every 2 crawling rounds** (during non-combat exploration)
- **After PCs make loud noises** (shouting, combat, breaking things)

**Roll:** 1d6. On a **1**, an encounter occurs.

---

## Scarlet Minotaur Scaling

**After the first encounter with the Scarlet Minotaur**, apply a **cumulative -2 penalty** to all future Random Encounters table rolls.

- **First d8 roll (before Minotaur met):** Roll normally (1-8)
- **Second d8 roll:** -2 (roll 1d8, subtract 2; treat results below 1 as 1)
- **Third d8 roll:** -4 (roll 1d8, subtract 4; treat results below 1 as 1)
- **After encounter with Minotaur:** Reset counter to 0

**Why?** The Minotaur is easier to find as it hunts more aggressively the longer it's searching.

## Creature Stat Blocks

### Scarlet Minotaur (Boss)

**AC:** 3 | **HP:** 45 | **ATK:** +5 (2d8+2 per attack, x4 on crit with Bloodlust)

**MV:** 40' (charging)

**Special Abilities:**
- **Unaware of secret doors**
- **Forgets unseen foes in 1d6 rounds**
- **Returns to Area 18 at least once per hour**
- **Pursues quarry relentlessly — never retreats**
- **Headbutt:** Can charge a target, knockdown save DC 15 STR

**Behavior:** Plows through halls looking for creatures to slay. Charges at shadows. Rage-filled.

---

### Beastman (Feral Humanoid)

**AC:** 6 | **HP:** 3-4 | **ATK:** +1 (1d4 claw or bite, or 1d6 if weapon)

**MV:** 30'

**Special Abilities:**
- **Dark Adaptation:** See in darkness
- **Pack Tactics:** Advantage on attack if ally is within 5' of target
- **Superstitious:** Terrified of loud noises, avoid making noise

**Behavior:** Avoid Scarlet Minotaur & Area 18. Retreat toward Area 14 or Area 23. Use secret doors. Lead enemies into bull statue traps. Will not leave hiding to help others.

**Treasure:** None usually. Occasionally carry 1d4 gp.

---

### Ettercap (Spider-Like Humanoid)

**AC:** 5 | **HP:** 4-5 | **ATK:** +2 (1d4 claw, 1d6 if weapon)

**MV:** 30' (can climb), 20' ceiling

**Special Abilities:**
- **Web:** Can spin webs (as Area 3)
- **Dark Adaptation:** See in darkness
- **Poison Web:** One target stuck, 1d4 damage/round. DC 12 DEX to escape
- **Ceiling Movement:** Move silently on ceilings, avoid bull statue traps

**Behavior:** Creep along ceilings. Unaware of secret doors. Reluctant to enter Area 18 (Minotaur). Distracted by treasure. Retreat to Area 4, gang up to assist allies.

**Treasure:** Greedy; carry loot (see Area 4).

---

### Wight (Undead Warrior)

**AC:** 4 | **HP:** 8 | **ATK:** +3 (1d8 weapon or touch attack 1d6 drain)

**MV:** 30'

**Special Abilities:**
- **Life Drain:** On hit, target loses 1d4 max HP (recovers after full rest)
- **Immune to mundane weapons** (only magic or blessed weapons work)
- **Darkvision:** See in complete darkness

**Behavior:** Arises from disturbed skeletons in Area 12. Aggressive. Wears **Scarab of Protection** (artifact).

---

### Darkmantle (Flying Terror)

**AC:** 6 | **HP:** 2 | **ATK:** +1 (1d4 peck or constrict)

**MV:** 30' (flying)

**Special Abilities:**
- **Blindness Aura:** Creatures within near are blinded (DC 12 WIS negates)
- **Darkness:** Can create area of magical darkness (as *darkness* spell)

**Behavior:** Territorial. Swoop & bob in warning dances. Avoid light sources.

---

### Cave Creeper (Insectoid Horror)

**AC:** 5 | **HP:** 6 | **ATK:** +2 (1d6 bite or 1d4 pincers)

**MV:** 40' (can climb walls & ceilings)

**Special Abilities:**
- **Adhesive Bite:** Target stuck in place until freed (DC 12 STR)
- **Pheromone Scent:** Can track via smell through dungeon
- **Acidic Saliva:** 1d4 damage per round while adhered

**Behavior:** Rushes toward light sources. Solitary hunter. Non-intelligent (acts on instinct).

---

### Ochre Jelly (Ooze)

**AC:** 8 | **HP:** 10 | **ATK:** +1 (1d6 engulf, 1d4 damage/round while engulfed)

**MV:** 10' (ooze movement, can climb)

**Special Abilities:**
- **Transparent:** DC 15 Perception to spot without disturbing floor mosaic
- **Non-Corporeal:** Immune to weapon damage (magic, fire, or cold works)
- **Acidic Body:** 1d4 damage/round to those inside

**Behavior:** Camouflaged in Area 20 mosaic. Lurks for prey.

## Simple Combat Quick Rules

**Turn Order:** All PCs go, then all enemies.

**Attack Roll:** d20 + ATK bonus vs. AC. Hit if equal/above target AC.

**Damage:** Roll damage die (e.g., 1d8) + modifier. Subtract from target HP.

**0 HP:** Character falls unconscious. Death timer starts: **1d4 + CON mod rounds** (min 1).

**Advantage/Disadvantage:** Roll 2d20, take higher (advantage) or lower (disadvantage).

---

## Trap Saves & Checks

**Failed Trap Save:** Takes full damage or effect (as noted in room description).

**Successful Save:** Halves damage or avoids effect entirely (DM's call).

**Common Save DCs:**
- **Easy (DC 9):** Obvious traps, simple escapes
- **Moderate (DC 12):** Normal traps, complex locks
- **Hard (DC 15):** Deadly traps, magical defenses
