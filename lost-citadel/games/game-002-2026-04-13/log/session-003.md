## The Minotaur Fight (Area 13)

**Bait setup:**
- Steve positioned at south end of hall, past bull statue.
- Sunscreen in alcove with Brell and Beetle.
- Grenton at north passage.

**Steve's taunt:**
> "HEY YOU BIG MONSTER! MY NAME IS STEVE! AND I'M IN LOVE WITH A WOMAN!"

- Minotaur heard, came charging from Area 12.

**Initiative:** Minotaur (17), Steve (14), Sunscreen (13), Beastmen (9).

**Round 1:**
- Minotaur charged into Area 13. Passed bull statue.
- **Bull Statue Trap triggered** (1d6 = 2, hit). 2d6 damage = 9. Minotaur knocked prone (failed STR save).
  - Minotaur HP: 45 → 36.
- Steve used Iron Bull trinket. Attacked Minotaur's right leg with longsword (Vash's tactic).
  - Attack: 14 + 4 = 18. HIT.
  - Damage: 7 + 3 = 10. **Leg crippled. Movement halved. Cannot charge.**
  - Minotaur HP: 36 → 27.
- Sunscreen dashed 15 ft, blew sleep powder in Minotaur's face (standing upwind).
  - Minotaur CON save DC 12: 8 + 3 = 11. FAILED.
  - **Minotaur unconscious for 4 rounds.**
- Grenton attacked: 15, +1 = 16. Hit. 4 damage (club). HP: 27 → 23.
- Brell attacked: 12, +1 = 13. Hit. 3 damage (dagger to throat). HP: 23 → 20.

**Round 2:**
- Minotaur unconscious. Skip.
- **Steve's coup de grâce:** Took longsword in both hands, two-handed swing at throat.
  - Attack with advantage (unconscious target) + Iron Bull: 18 + 4 = 22. **CRITICAL HIT (auto-crit on helpless).**
  - Damage max'd: 2d10 + 3 = 23 damage.
  - **Minotaur HP: 20 → -3. DEAD.**
- Sunscreen simultaneously tied Minotaur's legs with rope (precaution).
