## Turn 1: Area 1 — Mural Chamber (Revisited)

- Entered via SE doors. Sunscreen lit torch (timer: 6).
- Room unchanged except: hidden niche empty (horn taken last session), web-threads on NE ceiling glistening, drag marks toward east passage. Eska gone.

## Turn 2: Area 15 — Jar Storage (New)

- Long hallway west from Area 1 (~60 ft). Sulfur smell increasing.
- 20 fat terracotta jars, sealed with cork/wax. Faint squeaks and hisses inside.
- Sunscreen investigated a jar (DC 12 WIS, advantage, rolled 14 — success). Determined: thin clay, volatile chemical smell, dangerous to break carelessly.
- Sunscreen carefully opened one jar (DC 12 DEX, advantage, rolled 18 — success). Contents: grey ash, colorful thread doll, dried centipede husk. Grave offerings.
- South door carved: "R.I.P. Orwyn the Younger."

## Turn 3: Area 16 — Offering Bowls (New)

- Encounter check (2 rounds): 1d6 → 3. No encounter.
- Solemn hall with stone bowls on plinths. Murals of peasants offering gold/wheat.
- Steve listened (DC 12 WIS, rolled 15 — success). Heard human breathing, glass bottles clinking. Someone hiding behind SE bowl.
- Sunscreen spoke softly, offered peace.
- **NPC: Giuseppe Baldini** emerged. Herbalist, middle-aged, been trapped in citadel for days.
- Giuseppe shared intel:
  - Beastmen nest west through the labyrinth (15-20 of them, leader wants Minotaur dead)
  - Courtyard north is Minotaur's territory
  - Locked bronze door with sun carving somewhere east
  - Offering bowls grant blessings when you place offerings
- Steve searched room (DC 12 WIS, rolled 9 — fail). Nothing else found.
- **Steve placed 1 gp in offering bowl → Luck Token gained.**
- Party agreed to escort Giuseppe out for information.

## Turn 4: Escort Giuseppe to Exit

- Returned through Areas 15, 1, out SE doors.
- Giuseppe gave Steve an **antitoxin vial** as guide's fee.
- Giuseppe advised seeking beastmen alliance. Departed for town.

## Turn 5: Re-entry — Area 1

- **Sunscreen placed 1 gp in offering bowl (Area 16) → Luck Token gained.**
- Fresh torch lit (timer: 6).
- Headed west to Area 15, south to Area 16, west toward beastmen.

## Turn 6: Area 22 — Labyrinth (New)

- Narrow halls, 20' ceilings, hot wind. Sand and dust.
- **Cloven hoofprints** — two handspans wide, fresh. Minotaur has been through recently.
- Hoofprints trail south, away from party.
- Player initially headed north — Sunscreen reminded Steve that's the courtyard.

## Turn 7: Scouting the Courtyard

- Steve listened north (DC 12 WIS, rolled 11 — fail). Wind masked sounds.
- Sunscreen observed (DC 12 WIS, advantage, rolled 17 — success). Courtyard appears empty. Fresh gouges on pillars, hoofprints leading east. Minotaur left recently.

## Turn 8: Area 18 — Central Courtyard (New)

- Stealth entry. Sunscreen (advantage, rolled 17), Steve (rolled 13). Both pass.
- 50' × 50' open-air courtyard. 20' onyx bull statue with 30 skulls on horns. Bones, blood-stained sand, broken weapons everywhere.
- **Round 1 sifting:** Minotaur return check 1d6 (on 1) → 4. Safe.
- Sunscreen found **Mithral Shield** (prowling tiger carving, 40 gp).
- Decided not to push luck. Grabbed shield and headed south.

## Turn 9: Area 23 — Beastmen Lair (New)

- West through labyrinth. Animal smell intensifying. Claw marks on walls.
- **15 beastmen** in grimy chamber. Rogath (leader) on rat pelt mat.
- Rogath recognized Steve as the one who freed beastman from Area 2.
- Steve proposed alliance. Rogath agreed: kill the Minotaur, get treasure and safe passage.
- **The Plan:** Bait the Minotaur into bull statue traps. Steve and Sunscreen are bait. Beastmen use secret doors to escape.
- **Assigned warriors:** Brell (secret doors), Beetle (trap knowledge), Grenton (discipline).
- Alliance formed. Handshake.

## Turn 10: Return to Town

- Exited via Areas 22, 16, 15, 1, SE doors.
- Torch died on exit. Perfect timing.
