# Session 001 — Game-002

**Date:** 2026-04-13
**Party:** Steve (Fighter), Sunscreen (Thief)
**Duration:** 7 turns
**Session Summary:** First descent into the Lost Citadel. Party explored 4 rooms, recovered 175 gp in treasure, nearly lost Steve to a bull statue fire trap. Escaped via broken stairwell and returned to town.

---

## Turn Log

**Turn 1: Area 1 — Mural Chamber**
- Steve lights torch. Torch timer starts at 6.
- Steve listens — WIS check d20=8+0=8 vs DC 12. Fail. Can't hear past own heartbeat.
- Steve searches for treasure — INT check d20=14-1=13 vs DC 12. Success!
  - Found: **Silver bull horn (30 gp)** in hidden niche behind bull statue.
- Sunscreen follows. Notices something shifting on ceiling (northeast corner).
  - Sunscreen perception — WIS check d20=7+0=7 vs DC 12. Fail. Dismissed as shadows.
  - (Eska the ettercap hiding on ceiling, undetected)
- Torch timer: 5 rounds.

**Turn 2: Area 2 — Blinding Cave**
- Party moves south into Area 2.
- Quartz walls reflect torchlight — blinding flash.
  - Steve DEX check d20=15+0=15 vs DC 12. Success — shields eyes in time.
  - Sunscreen DEX check d20=6+2=8 vs DC 12. Fail — **blinded for 3 rounds.**
- Room contains 5 man-sized white silk bundles (cocoons) in corners.
- Torch timer: 4 rounds.
- Encounter check (2 rounds elapsed) — d6=4. No encounter.

**Turn 3: Area 2 — Investigating bundles**
- Sunscreen listens (blinded) — WIS check d20=16+0=16 vs DC 12. Success!
  - Hears skittering above — something on the ceiling (Eska).
  - Warns Steve.
- Sunscreen panics — flails and kicks wildly. **Loud noise.**
- Steve investigates breathing bundle — INT check d20=13-1=12 vs DC 12. Success.
  - Finds living beastman inside cocoon. Emaciated, terrified. Speaks unknown language.
- Eska hisses from ceiling, agitated by noise.
- Encounter check (loud noise) — d6=5. No additional encounter.
- Sunscreen: 2 rounds blindness remaining. Torch timer: 3 rounds.

**Turn 4: Area 2 — Freeing beastman**
- Steve frees beastman — STR check d20=12+2=14 vs DC 9. Easy success.
  - Beastman too weak to stand. Grateful/terrified.
- Steve looks up at ceiling creature — WIS check d20=17+0=17 vs DC 12. Success!
  - Sees Eska clearly: purple-grey, weaselly, potbellied humanoid (ettercap). Clutching a pearl. Hostile. Retreats toward Area 1.
- Sunscreen flails toward south exit — blindness clears at south passage.
  - Sees passage sloping down toward Area 7.
- Sunscreen blindness ended. Torch timer: 2 rounds.

**Turn 5: Area 7 — Bull Statue (Trap)**
- Party enters Area 7 together.
- Bronze bull statue — life-sized, rearing, mouth open. Scorch marks on floor. Burned hair smell.
- Steve reads wall scratching — INT check d20=10-1=9 vs DC 9. Success. Reads: "DON'T TOUCH"
- Sunscreen trap scan (advantage) — d20=8,14+2=16 vs DC 12. Success!
  - Spots pressure plate in front of bull's mouth. Warns Steve.
- Floor scratching near south exit: arrow pointing south with word "SAFE."
- Sunscreen says "SAFE" is a trap, suggests east exit instead.
- Torch timer: **1 round remaining.** Flame guttering.

**Turn 6: Area 7 — Torch expires / relight**
- Steve's torch dies. **Total darkness.**
- Steve lights second torch. Timer reset to 6. **Steve has no more torches.**
- Steve searches room — INT check d20=6-1=5 vs DC 12. Fail. Finds nothing.
- Sunscreen searches (advantage) — d20=11,18+1=19 vs DC 12. Success!
  - Spots narrow east passage to Area 10.
  - Finds arrow + "SAFE" scratched near south exit (same crude hand as warning).
- Encounter check (2 rounds) — d6=3. No encounter.
- Torch timer: 5 rounds.

**Turn 7: Area 10 — Bull Statue (Trap) / THE EMERALD**
- Party moves east to Area 10. Another bronze bull statue — same trap setup.
- Fresh scorch marks. Charred bone fragments near pressure plate.
- Sunscreen trap scan (advantage) — d20=15,9+2=17 vs DC 12. Spots pressure plate.
- Sunscreen retrieves gold ring from charred bones — DEX check d20=13+2=15 vs DC 9. Success.
  - Found: **Gold ring (25 gp).**
- Skeleton impaled on bull's horns. **Emerald visible** in bull's forehead behind it.
- Sunscreen spots it (advantage) — d20=17,12+1=18 vs DC 12. Success.

**Steve climbs for the emerald:**
- Avoids pressure plate — DEX d20=14+0=14 vs DC 12. Success.
- Climbs bull (Grit advantage) — STR d20=7,16+2=18 vs DC 12. Success.
- Bull activation check — d6=4. Statue stays still.
- Pries emerald free (Grit advantage) — STR d20=5,11+2=13 vs DC 12. Success!
  - Found: **Emerald (120 gp).**
- Bull activation check — d6=2. **STATUE ACTIVATES.**
- Steve leaps clear — DEX d20=10+0=10 vs DC 15. **FAIL.**
- Hits pressure plate. Fire blast from bull's mouth.
  - **Damage: 2d6 = 7. Steve drops to 0 HP.**
- **Steve dying.** Death timer: 1d4+1 = 3 rounds.
- Sunscreen stabilizes Steve — INT check d20=20+1=21 vs DC 15. **Critical success.**
- Steve stabilized. Unconscious, 0 HP.
- Sunscreen drags Steve to east exit (broken stairwell) — STR d20=15+0=15 vs DC 12. Success.
- **Party exits citadel into daylight.**
- Full rest outside. Steve recovers 1d4=3 HP. Wakes at 3/6 HP.
- Party walks home to town.
